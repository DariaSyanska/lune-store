Shipping Policy

We offer worldwide shipping. All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

  • • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days) — € 15
  • • Express Shipping (2-3 business days) — € 30
  • • Free shipping on orders over € 500

Returns & Exchanges

Lune accepts returns within 14 days of receipt of your order. Items must be in original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at care@lune-store.com.
