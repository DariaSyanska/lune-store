Our Story

Born under the moonlight,
designed for the sun.

Founded in 2024, Lune was created for the modern woman who seeks elegance without compromise. We believe that true luxury lies in simplicity and the tactile sensation of the finest natural fabrics.

Slow Fashion

We don't follow trends. We create timeless pieces designed to last a lifetime, reducing waste and promoting conscious consumption.

Ethical Production

Every stitch is made in small, family-owned ateliers where craftsmanship and fair labor are the highest priorities.

Minimalism

Clean lines, neutral palettes, and effortless silhouettes. We strip away the noise to let your inner light shine.

Lune is not just clothing; it is a moonlit state of mind.

The Essence of Touch

01

OEKO-TEX® Certified Silk

Breathable, hypoallergenic, and incredibly soft. Our silk is sourced from ethical farms that prioritize the lifecycle of the silkworm.
